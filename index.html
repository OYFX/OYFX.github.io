Biography

Attained bachelor's and master's degrees in Industrial Design (National first-class major) at Xihua University with honor of Outstanding Graduate twice. Worked on diverse design projects, spanning furniture, electronics, user experience, and complex service systems. Garnered prestigious awards, including 2023 Red Dot Award, driven by penetrating insights, user-friendly design, and bold creativity. Actively participated in research projects targeting broader societal challenges, published five high-quality papers in peer-reviewed journals, one indexed in CSCD (China Science Citation Database).
